# The Peacock Conspiracy - Novel Development

A comprehensive novel development project for **"The Peacock Conspiracy"**, a mystery novel set in mid-1990s Los Angeles about a 19-year-old USC student who works at NBC News and Disneyland, and stumbles into a conspiracy surrounding the 1996 Olympic Park bombing.

## Project Structure

```
peacock/
├── prompt.md.txt                    # Original novel brief
├── index.html                       # Master project index (open this)
├── research/
│   └── research_catalog.html        # Comprehensive research catalog (300+ facts)
├── iterations/
│   ├── iter1/                       # Iteration 1: Three parallel drafts + critique
│   │   ├── draft_a_thriller.md      # Draft A: Thriller/Crichton approach
│   │   ├── draft_b_literary.md      # Draft B: Literary/character approach
│   │   ├── draft_c_journalism.md    # Draft C: Journalism-mechanics approach
│   │   ├── critique_iter1.md        # Critique of all three drafts
│   │   └── superbook_iter1.md       # Best-of synthesis from Iteration 1
│   ├── iter2/                       # Iteration 2: Refined drafts
│   ├── iter3/                       # Iteration 3: Final polish
│   └── final/                       # The definitive outline
└── README.md
```

## Iterative Process

1. **Research** -- Massive fact-finding on LA/US events 1994-1996
2. **Draft** -- Three parallel outlines from different angles (thriller, literary, journalism)
3. **Critique** -- Identify strengths/weaknesses of each
4. **Synthesize** -- Merge best ideas into a super-book
5. **Repeat** -- Three full iterations, each building on the last

## Key Research Topics

- OJ Simpson Trial (1994-1995)
- 1996 Atlanta Olympics & NBC's $456M broadcast rights
- Centennial Olympic Park Bombing & Richard Jewell
- NBC's Must-See TV empire (Friends, Seinfeld, ER)
- Rise of Fox News (October 1996)
- Tabloidization of broadcast journalism
- KNBC Channel 4 / NBC Los Angeles newsroom
- USC campus life 1994-1996
- Disneyland's Castle Heraldry Shoppe
- 1994 Northridge Earthquake
- 1996 Presidential Election (Clinton vs. Dole)
- LA crime, celebrity culture, quirky local stories
